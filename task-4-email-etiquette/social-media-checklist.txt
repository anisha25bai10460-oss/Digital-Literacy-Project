Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist


Do's (Best Practices)
* Audit your privacy settings regularly to control who can see your personal posts and photos.
* Use a professional profile picture and bio on platforms like LinkedIn to attract recruiters.
* Think before you share content, considering how it might look to a future employer or professor.
* Network with industry professionals and join academic groups related to your AIML specialisation.
* Credit original creators when sharing articles, research, or media that isn't your own work.


Don'ts (Avoid These)
* Don't post sensitive personal info like your home address, phone number, or Aadhaar details publicly.
* Don't engage in online arguments or use aggressive language that could damage your reputation.
* Don't share unverified news or "fake news" that could spread misinformation within your community.
* Don't post photos of illegal activities or content that violates your university's code of conduct.
* Don't ignore cyberbullying—avoid participating in it and report it if you see it happening to others.
